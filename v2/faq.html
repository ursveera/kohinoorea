What is Kohinoor EA?

Kohinoor EA is a premium automated trading system developed for MetaTrader 4.

Does it work on MT4?

Yes. It is built specifically for MetaTrader 4 and MT4-compatible broker accounts.

Which pair does it support?

It is optimized for XAUUSD (Gold).

Is it fully automated?

Yes. It can monitor the market, place trades, and manage positions automatically.

Do I need a VPS?

Not always, but it is strongly recommended for stable operation and lower interruption risk.

Can I change risk settings?

Yes. Users can customize risk and trading preferences.

Does it work on all brokers?

It is designed for MT4-compatible broker accounts, but broker conditions can affect real execution.

Minimum deposit?

The minimum deposit still depends on broker conditions and your tested operating range.

Refund policy?

Due to instant digital delivery, refunds are generally not available unless a verified technical issue cannot be resolved.
